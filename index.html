How AccessiScan Works

1. Generate Personas

Our Nemotron-4 340B model creates 1,000–10,000 synthetic users based on real-world accessibility profiles — age, disability, device, literacy, and more.

2. Simulate Interactions

Each persona “uses” your site: clicking buttons, reading text, navigating menus — evaluated against WCAG 2.2, EN 301 549, and user behavior models.

3. Get Actionable Reports

Receive a clear, visual report showing what works — and what fails — for each group. Get prioritized, plain-language fixes: “Increase button size to 48px for elderly users.”
